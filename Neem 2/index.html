Who we are?

Neem Foundation is founded by a team of development professionals, working in the development sectors for more than a decade. We gained expertised in developing social development project proposals, its Implementation and management, innovating solutions on social issues, using digital technologies in social sector, supporting organization development processes, and conducting capacity building under different thematic spaces and IEC development The team members have gathered experiences through working with grass-root level organizations. Our team members have worked with renowned development partners, National and International donor agencies and as technical experts with Government flagship programmes. Working with the sector, the team has realized that there is pertinent need of an active resource agency which can provide techno managerial and advisory support to the Civil Society Organizations (CSOs), donor agencies, technical and consulting agencies, academic institutions and CSRs. Based on our experiences and expertise as a Resource and Implementation Agency, we provide technical, management and advisory services to our clients, through consultancy agreements in the context of issues related to gender & agency building, Health & Nutrition, adolescent & women empowerment, livelihood linkages, local self-governance, education, research and other related social issues, while contributing in quality implementation and in achieving the project results in a best possible way. We also explore new ways of implementation, MIS, reporting and documentation through technology. Post Covid-19, the team came together and started this initiative. We will be able to win trust by ensuring quality in services and products, placing a high value on the partnerships with clients and consultants, as well as the communities we work with. As an organization we follow high level of integrity in all assignments and deliverables. We believe in innovation to address developmental issues by looking beyond the boundaries. We have a strong network of credible CSOs, Development Partners and Government Institutions who collaborate with us to work on multidisciplinary assignments.
